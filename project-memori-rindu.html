Memori Rindu

Role: Animation, Lighting Tools: Maya, After Effects Year: 2025 Duration: 3–4 min

Overview

Memori Rindu is an emotional short about nostalgia and family. I focused on shot planning, animation polish, lighting, and color mood to support the storytelling beats.

  • LookDev & lighting for evening interiors
  • Camera blocking and shot continuity
  • Render passes and compositing in After Effects

Process

  1. Story & Boards — Drafted beats, emotional arc, and transitions.
  2. Blocking — Established camera angles, timing, and silhouettes.
  3. Spline & Polish — Clean arcs, secondary motion, and facial poses.
  4. Lighting & Color — Warm evening palette for nostalgia; rim lights for focus.
  5. Compositing — Grain, bloom, and subtle vignette to unify renders.
